## Regression hypotheses ‚Äî slots endpoint (Opus code-read, `api/public/slots/route.ts`)

Symptom at demo: "after adding that customer's services, can't find a time slot / can't book." Ranked causes:

1. **business_hours shape/key mismatch** (route.ts:23-25) ‚Äî `getSlots` reads `businessHours["sunday".."saturday"]` and needs `.open===true`. If a newly set-up business stores hours under different keys or shape, every day ‚Üí `[]`. **Most likely.**
2. **Infinite hang on duration 0/null** (route.ts:39) ‚Äî `for (t=open; t+dur<=close; t+=dur+buffer)`. If a service saved with `duration=0/null` AND `buffer=0`, `t` never advances ‚Üí request hangs ‚Üí page stuck "finding slots." Verify the settings form cannot save a service without a positive duration; consider a guard (`if (durationMinutes<=0) return []`).
3. **NaN duration** (route.ts:52) ‚Äî `parseInt(get("duration")||"30")`: a non-numeric non-empty value ‚Üí NaN (no fallback) ‚Üí zero slots. Check what the client sends for a service with an add-on/nested option.
4. **staffChoice path** (route.ts:139-171) ‚Äî `allow_staff_choice` on + empty eligible staff pool, or staff `working_hours` shape mismatch ‚Üí union empty.

Worker on view 2 (`/[slug]` slots): confirm which symptom (empty response vs hung request vs error) via canary, then systematic-debugging.
